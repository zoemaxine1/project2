Natural Vistas


From calm parks to quiet trails and spots by the water, these places are great for escaping and enjoying some nature.
If you're looking for somewhere to chill and take in the surroundings, this list has you covered.

Sutro Baths

1004 Point Lobos Ave, San Francisco, CA 94121
With stunning coastal views, this spot is right next to Seal Rock Beach. Here, you can explore the ruins of the old Sutro Baths and even walk through a cave, offering endless opportunities for adventure and discovery.

Devil's Slide Bunker

5981 California Coastal Trl, Pacifica, CA 94044 
Devil’s Bunker is a little outside of San Francisco, but totally worth the drive. The place has an old military bunker to check out, plus hidden paths that lead to some amazing views. It’s a cool mix of history and nature, offering a peaceful escape from the city.

Mount Tamalpais

Mount Tamalpais State Park, Stinson Beach, CA  94970 37.92080° N, 122.63260° W
Ever wanted to feel like you're above the clouds? This spot, only a short scenic drive away, lets you do just that. With easy parking right at the location, Mount Tam has a ton of great spots to park and take in the views, and this is one of the best
